About me

Email: ngernest@alumni.upenn.edu
GitHub: ngernest

Hello! I recently graduated from the University of Pennsylvania, where I obtained a Master's in Computer Science & a Bachelor's in Math & CS.

My academic interests include functional programming in OCaml & Haskell, the Coq proof assistant, compilers and type systems. I also enjoy thinking about abstract algebra & graph theory.

At Penn, I was fortunate to be able to do programming languages research as part of PLClub:

  • I am currently working on Mica, an automated tool for conducting property-based testing of OCaml modules, advised by Professor Benjamin Pierce and Harry Goldstein. I presented Mica at the ICFP ’23 Student Research Competition.
  • I also worked on a team mechanizing type soundness proofs for the Hindley-Milner type system in Coq, advised by Professor Stephanie Weirich.

Feel free to visit the projects page for further details!
